It goes without saying, books are our teachers and friends.

They teach us to be kind, clever, polite, hardworking, friendly.

Books help us to learn more about nature,
the world around us and many other interesting things.

There are a lot of books on history, about animals,
travellers, children, school and so on.

We must keep books clean and tidy. We must not spoil them.

I am fond of reading, too.
